In New Horizons, the total number of bugs is 80 up from 72 in New Leaf. New bugs include the atlas moth, paper kite butterfly, Madagascan sunset moth, damselfly, blue weevil beetle, common bluebottle, earth-boring dung beetle, man-faced stink bug, and rosalia batesi beetle. Dropped bugs include the fruit beetle, petaltail dragonfly, house centipede, oak silk moth, and the lantern fly.
