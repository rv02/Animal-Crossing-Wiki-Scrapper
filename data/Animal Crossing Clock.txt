Animal Crossing Clock is a DSiWare application. As with the Animal Crossing Calculator, it is 200 Nintendo Points for the Nintendo DSi. It can also be purchased on the Nintendo 3DS for $1.99 from the Nintendo eShop.

This software is able to keep track of time, the player can use a handclock (seen in City Folk) or a digital clock (seen in Animal Forest e+). The player can make a Town Tune and choose from 3 Animal Crossing related alarms.
