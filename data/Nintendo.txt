Nintendo Co., Ltd. (任天堂株式会社 Nintendō Kabushiki-gaisha) is a Japanese software publisher, video game developer, and hardware manufacturer. Nintendo is one of the largest video game companies with numerous successful franchises, including Mario, The Legend of Zelda, Kirby, Metroid, Star Fox, and Animal Crossing.

Since Nintendo develops and publishes the Animal Crossing series, some Nintendo products and items based on other Nintendo franchises have appeared in Animal Crossing games.
