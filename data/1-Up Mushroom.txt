The 1-Up Mushroom is a miscellaneous item from the 1.8.0 Super Mario update in New Horizons. It can be obtained from Nook Shopping for 2,000  starting March 1st, and it can be sold at Nook's Cranny for 500 . It has no variations and cannot be customized.
