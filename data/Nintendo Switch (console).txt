The Nintendo Switch is the successor to both the Nintendo Wii U and Nintendo 3DS. It is a hybrid console that can be used as both a stationary and portable device, allowing users to play where they like. The console was officially revealed on October 20, 2016 in a trailer that demonstrated its hybrid capabilities. This console's version of Animal Crossing is New Horizons.
