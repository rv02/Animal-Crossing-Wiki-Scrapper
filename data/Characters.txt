Category:Characters is a list of articles about individual characters who appeared in at least one Animal Crossing game. This list does not include characters seen in Super Smash Bros. Other categories are available for lists of characters who are only in a certain game, made their first appearances in a particular title, or can be further subdivided into villagers, visitors, and special residents.
